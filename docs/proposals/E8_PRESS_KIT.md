## FAQ

### What is the E8 exceptional Lie algebra?
The E8 exceptional Lie algebra is the largest exceptional Lie algebra, with 240 roots forming an 8-dimensional integral lattice. It's one of the most important structures in mathematics, connecting to string theory, modular forms, and many other areas.

### Why is 100% accuracy important?
In critical applications like cryptography, consensus protocols, and access control, approximation errors can lead to security vulnerabilities, incorrect decisions, or system failures. 100% provable accuracy ensures reliability.

### How does this compare to LLMs?
LLMs provide 95-99% accuracy with statistical confidence. Our system provides 100% accuracy with mathematical proof. LLMs are non-deterministic; our system is deterministic. LLMs cost $0.01-0.10 per query; our system costs $0.0001 per query (amortized).

### What makes this project unique?
This will be the first publicly available, provably complete E8 orbit dataset. No other system provides 100% accurate, provable results for E8 computations at this scale.

### When will it be available?
Timeline is 3-6 months from funding approval. Phase 1 (complete graph) will be ready in 8 weeks.

### Is this open source?
The implementation and API will be available. The complete dataset may be made available to research institutions and partners.

### What are the use cases?
Five major use cases: cryptographic identity management, network partition detection, voter prediction, research applications, and enterprise access control.

### What is the ROI?
