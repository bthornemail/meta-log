### 2.2 Multiplication Rules from Fano Plane

The 7 points of the Fano plane correspond to the 7 **imaginary octonion units** e_1, e_2, ..., e_7.

The multiplication rules are encoded by the lines:
- If you traverse a line in the direction of its arrow: e_i * e_j = e_k (positive)
- If you traverse backwards: e_i * e_j = -e_k (negative)
- All imaginary units square to -1: e_i^2 = -1

**Example using line {1,2,4}**:
- e_1 * e_2 = e_4
- e_2 * e_4 = e_1  
- e_4 * e_1 = e_2
- But e_2 * e_1 = -e_4 (reversed direction)

The Fano plane **completely determines** octonion multiplication.
